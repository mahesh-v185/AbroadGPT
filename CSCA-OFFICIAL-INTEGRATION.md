# CSCA Official Syllabus Integration

## 📋 Overview

This document outlines the integration of the official China Scholarship Council (CSCA) examination syllabi into the CSCA PREP WEB platform. The platform has been updated to align with the official 2025 Edition syllabi for Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.

## 🎯 Examination Format (Official)

- **Duration**: 60 minutes
- **Total Score**: 100 points
- **Language**: Chinese or English
- **Question Type**: Multiple Choice (Single Answer)
- **Number of Questions**: 48 questions

## 📚 Track Structure Update

### Track 1: CSCA Core Sciences (Updated)

Previously: Computer Science Core Areas
**Now**: CSCA Core Sciences - Official China Scholarship Council Subjects

#### Mathematics (4 Modules)
1. **Sets and Inequalities**
   - Definition, operations, representation of sets
   - Basic properties and solution methods of inequalities
   - Estimated time: 60 minutes

2. **Functions**
   - Concepts and properties (domain, range, monotonicity, parity)
   - Basic elementary functions (power, exponential, logarithmic, trigonometric)
   - Sequences (arithmetic and geometric)
   - Basics of derivatives and calculus
   - Estimated time: 90 minutes

3. **Geometry and Algebra**
   - Plane analytic geometry (lines, circles, ellipses, hyperbolas, parabolas)
   - Vectors and complex numbers
   - Solid geometry (rectangular coordinate system in space)
   - Estimated time: 90 minutes

4. **Probability and Statistics**
   - Classical probability model and probability calculation
   - Numerical characteristics of data (mean, variance)
   - Basic concepts of normal distribution
   - Estimated time: 60 minutes

#### Physics (5 Modules)
1. **Mechanics**
   - Kinematics: displacement, velocity, acceleration, free fall
   - Newton's laws of motion and applications
   - Momentum and impulse, conservation of momentum
   - Work and energy, conservation of mechanical energy
   - Circular motion and universal gravitation
   - Simple harmonic motion and mechanical waves
   - Estimated time: 120 minutes

2. **Electromagnetism**
   - Electrostatics: Coulomb's law, electric field, electric potential
   - Direct current circuits: Ohm's law, series and parallel circuits
   - Magnetic field: magnetic induction, Ampere's force, Lorentz force
   - Electromagnetic induction: Faraday's law, Lenz's law
   - Estimated time: 120 minutes

3. **Thermodynamics**
   - Kinetic theory of gases
   - Ideal gas equation of state
   - First law of thermodynamics
   - Estimated time: 60 minutes

4. **Optics**
   - Geometrical optics: laws of reflection and refraction
   - Physical optics: interference and diffraction
   - Estimated time: 60 minutes

5. **Modern Physics**
   - Photoelectric effect
   - Atomic structure
   - Fundamentals of nuclear physics
   - Estimated time: 60 minutes

#### Chemistry (4 Modules)
1. **Basic Chemical Concepts and Calculations**
   - Classification and state changes of matter
   - Chemical notation and equation writing
   - Solution concentration and pH calculations
   - Calculations involving amount of substance
   - Application of ideal gas law
   - Estimated time: 90 minutes

2. **Properties and Reactions of Substances**
   - Properties of common inorganic substances
   - Basic organic compounds (hydrocarbons and derivatives)
   - Identification of redox reactions
   - Ionic reactions and testing methods
   - Estimated time: 120 minutes

3. **Chemical Theories and Laws**
   - Atomic structure and periodic law
   - Chemical bonds and intermolecular forces
   - Reaction rate and chemical equilibrium
   - Theories of electrolyte solutions
   - Estimated time: 120 minutes

4. **Chemical Experiments and Applications**
   - Laboratory safety and use of apparatus
   - Preparation and identification of common gases
   - Methods for separation and purification of substances
   - Analysis of industrial chemical processes (e.g., ammonia synthesis)
   - Estimated time: 90 minutes
